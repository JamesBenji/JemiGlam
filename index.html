About  JemiGlam

Jemi Glam is a beauty store in Kampala, Uganda, catering to corporate ladies, university students, wedding parties, celebrities, influencers, and bloggers.

The business aims to provide customized makeup services and makeup accessories to its clientele. Its primary objectives include reaching potential customers effectively, facilitating easy access to services, ensuring customer satisfaction, and streamlining business operations.

MISSION

To be the leading makeup brand and manufacturer of makeup products

VISION

To provide unique lip products and natural makeup for customers at affordable rates

CORE VALUES

Core values customer centred, high quality services and reliability and accessibility of the services offered
